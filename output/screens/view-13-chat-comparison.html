What's the difference between the traditional Hainanese chicken and a quick weeknight version?

Great question! Here's a side-by-side comparison of what changes and why it matters:

🐌 2h+

For a Tuesday night the quick version is genuinely excellent. For a dinner party — invest in the traditional. The silk skin and perfumed rice are what make it special.
💡 The single biggest upgrade for the quick version
Don't skip the ice bath. 30 seconds in ice water after cooking completely transforms the texture of the skin — even for pressure-cooked chicken thighs.
🧊 Fill a large bowl with cold water and ice cubes before you start cooking. When chicken is done, plunge it in immediately for 30 seconds, then pat dry.
Two more quick wins for the weeknight version:
🧄
Fry ginger in sesame oil
Add it to your stock-cube rice — it makes a huge difference to the aroma
🧅
Make the ginger sauce
Even from a jar of minced ginger — it's the heart of the dish and only takes 2 minutes
