Ingredients
Chicken thigh, sliced 400g
Coconut milk 200ml
Green curry paste 3 tbsp
Thai basil leaves 1 cup
Red bell pepper, sliced 1
Fish sauce 2 tbsp
Instructions
1
Heat coconut milk in a wok over medium heat. Add green curry paste and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
2
Add sliced chicken and cook for 5 minutes until no longer pink. Stir in fish sauce.
3
Add bell pepper and simmer for 3 minutes. Remove from heat, fold in Thai basil. Serve over jasmine rice.
