Any pro tips for making Hainanese Chicken even better?
m.
Absolutely! Here are the three levels of doing this dish, from weeknight to restaurant quality:
⚡
QUICK HACK
Use store-bought rotisserie chicken and just make the proper ginger rice and sauces. Still excellent, 10 minutes of active work.
👨‍🍳
CHEF'S METHOD
Blanch the chicken in boiling water for 10 seconds, then immediately transfer to an ice bath. Repeat 3 times. This firms the skin and removes any impurities for a cleaner flavour.
✨
PRO VARIATION
Instead of plain poaching liquid, make a proper court-bouillon with aromatics. Adds enormous depth to both the chicken and the rice cooked in the stock.
The ice bath trick (tip 2) is probably the biggest single upgrade you can make. It's the difference between home cooking and what you'd eat at a hawker centre in Singapore.
