Thai Green Curry

⏱ 35 min
🔥 420 cal

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh, sliced 400g
  • Coconut milk 200ml
  • Green curry paste 3 tbsp
  • Thai basil leaves 1 cup
  • Red bell pepper, sliced 1
  • Fish sauce 2 tbsp
  • Palm sugar 1 tbsp
  • Jasmine rice, for serving to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Heat coconut milk in a wok over medium heat. Add green curry paste and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

  2. 2

    Add sliced chicken and cook for 5 minutes until no longer pink. Stir in fish sauce and palm sugar.

  3. 3

    Add bell pepper and simmer for 3 minutes. Remove from heat, fold in Thai basil. Serve over jasmine rice.

  4. 4

    While curry simmers, cook jasmine rice according to package instructions.

  5. 5

    Taste and adjust seasoning — add more fish sauce for salt, lime juice for brightness, sugar for sweetness.

  6. 6

    Garnish with fresh Thai basil and sliced red chili. Serve immediately.
