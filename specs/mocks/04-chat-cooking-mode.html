    Step 1 — Heat the wok

    Heat 200ml coconut milk in a wok over medium heat. Add 3 tbsp green curry paste and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

    💡

    Tip: The paste is fragrant when you can smell the lemongrass and galangal — about 90 seconds on medium.

    Step 3 — Add vegetables

    Add 1 red bell pepper, sliced and 100g bamboo shoots to the wok. Reduce to a gentle simmer and cook for 3 minutes until the pepper softens slightly but retains its crunch.

    💡

    Tip: Don't overcook — bell pepper should still have a slight bite. It'll keep cooking after you turn off the heat.
