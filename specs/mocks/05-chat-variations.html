  5. What's the proper way to make this curry?

  6. The traditional technique has a few key steps that most shortcuts miss:

    👨‍🍳

    Traditional Technique

    • Fry the curry paste dry first before adding coconut milk — this intensifies the aroma

    • Use the top cream from a can of coconut milk first; reserve the thinner milk for later

    • Blanch vegetables briefly in salted water before adding to the curry for better texture

  7. The base starts with a proper — the French term for a mix of onion, carrot, and celery sautéed together to build flavor depth.

    Mirepoix

    French

    A culinary base of diced onion (2 parts), carrot (1 part), and celery (1 part), cooked in butter to build flavor.

  12. Interesting fact about Thai Green Curry:

    📜

    Thai Green Curry originated in Central Thailand in the early 20th century. The green color comes from young green chilis, not green bell peppers as Western adaptations suggest.
